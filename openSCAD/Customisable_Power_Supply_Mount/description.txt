Customisable Power Supply Mount (parametric)

A customisable / parametric mount used to screw power supplies and other cuboid items to walls and the underside of shelves.

Dimensions of some examples:
Game Cube:
POWER_SUPPLY_WIDTH = 65.6;
POWER_SUPPLY_HEIGHT = 46.8;

Xbox 360: (first generation, not sure if it's changed for later models)
POWER_SUPPLY_WIDTH = 77.2;
POWER_SUPPLY_HEIGHT = 52.5;

Dell ATX: (note this is a slightly different size to other ATX power supplies)
POWER_SUPPLY_WIDTH = 151.0;
POWER_SUPPLY_HEIGHT = 98.1;
